## 👩‍💼 About Me

> *"Empowering learners with the technologies of tomorrow — today."*

I am **Anita Budhiraja**, Associate Dean – Academics at **NIELIT Chandigarh/Ropar**, with over **25 years of rich teaching and training experience** in Computer Science & Emerging Technologies. I specialize in bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry relevance across AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, Blockchain, and Software Engineering.

- 🏛️ **Role:** Associate Dean – Academics, NIELIT Ropar
- 🎓 **Qualifications:** M.Tech (C Level, NIELIT) · MCA (B Level, DOEACC) · PGDCA (KUK) · M.A. Economics (Punjab University)
- 📍 **Location:** Chandigarh / Ropar, India
- 📞 **Contact:** +91 98159 88717
